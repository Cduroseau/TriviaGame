Looney Tunes Trivia

Welcome to The Warner Bros' Looney Tunes Trivia Game. These questions will test your knowledge on everything pertaining to Looney Tunes. Come see where you rank among your peers when it comes to Bugs Bunny and his crew.

This game has 2 levels. The easy level consist of 10 questions with an over all time limit of 5 minutes. The hard level consist of 10 difficult questions with a time limit of 20 seconds for each questions.
